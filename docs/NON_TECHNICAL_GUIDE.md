### ✨ UtahMosphere: The "It Just Works" Cloud

#### **What is UtahMosphere?**
UtahMosphere is a new way to run your websites and apps without paying monthly rent to giant tech companies like Google or Amazon. 

#### **Why should I care?**
- **Save Money:** You buy a small device (like a Mini PC) once, and that's it. No more $500 monthly bills for your business website.
- **Privacy:** Your data stays in your office or home. Not on a server in another country.
- **Reliability:** If the internet goes down, your local apps still work. If one device breaks, the others in your network keep everything running.
- **Easy to Use:** You don't need to be a computer genius. You can talk to it! "Butler, update my store prices" and it happens.

#### **How do I get started?**
1.  **Get a Brick:** Buy a Mini PC or a Raspberry Pi.
2.  **Run the Setup:** A technician (or a tech-savvy friend) runs one command to install the UtahMosphere brain.
3.  **Deploy your App:** Tell the system where your website code is, and it handles the rest. No more complicated settings!

**Step-by-step tutorial:** [Setup Without Jargon](tutorials/06-non-technical-setup.md)

#### **The "No-Maintenance" Promise**
UtahMosphere is designed to heal itself. It cleans up its own mess, fixes its own errors, and optimizes its own memory. You set it up once and forget about it. It just works.
